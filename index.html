About SketchCature


By April and May

April and May have known each other since 5 th grade and are good friends until now that they have become teenagers. They share a lot of things in common from the dresses they wear, the places they love to visit, and the food they eat to the music that they like listening into. In fact they are both avid fans of the Korean boy band BTS and consider themselves as die hard BTS armies. Since they did not come from well-financed family, they thought of starting a small business on the onset of the pandemic to cope with their families financial needs. April being good in drawing and May being good with marketing things thought about selling sketches of BTS members that are mass produced on sintra boards. They called the business SketchCature.
